About
Here's a little bit about me!

I am a recent graduate from Fordham University with a degree in New Media and Digital Design with a concentration in Art, Text, and Design with a minor in Economics.

I've mainly practiced pencil drawing, and now I am exploring different digital mediums for design while I have the opportunity. I draw inspiration from everything I enjoy: from outer space, from theater, from my pets, etc. Please Enjoy! :)
